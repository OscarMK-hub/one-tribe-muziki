About Us

One Tribe Muziki is a collective of artists, musicians, and creatives who believe in the power of music to unite people across borders, cultures, and generations.

What We Do

  • Live Performances: Energetic and soulful shows blending traditional African rhythms with modern sounds.
  • Music Production: Original songs, collaborations, and studio recordings.
  • Workshops: Music education, drumming sessions, and community outreach programs.
  • Cultural Exchange: Showcasing global heritage through artistic collaboration.
